# 🧬 AI Studio

The workshop where Flowork builds and prunes itself.
- **⚡ Design** — describe what you want and it generates a module or agent.
- **Pending** — anything it generated waits here for your call: **⏍ Approve** or **⊗ Reject**. Nothing
  lands without you.
- **Reaper** — apoptosis. It lists modules that look dead or flagged, and you can **⊘ Reap** them
  (remove). Owner-gated, so the AI can't quietly delete things.

**Principle:** the system can grow new parts and shed dead ones, but a human always holds the approve
button. Build with a conscience; clean up without fear.
